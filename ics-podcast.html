ICS Podcast

Started in 2018, the ICS Podcast provides updates on ICSSC's activities in a new and creative fashion. Each episode features one or multiple board members discussing topics ranging from upcoming events to personal experiences. Check out the playlist here, and follow our Facebook page to stay updated for future episodes!

Episode 5

December 8, 2019

In this episode of the ICS Podcast, the board of ICS Student Council gives some of their thoughts on their time in the organization, advice as students, and some details on upcoming events and activities! It's our first serious podcast in a long time, so you better seriously listen to it.
